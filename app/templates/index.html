In short a non-written language native speaker registers on the application and logs in. He/she then chooses what images to label with vocal audio snippets, he/she could even upload his/her own images. The audio snippets are converted to the following formats MFCC | STFT | spectral centroid | zero crossing and stored inside a knowledge graph. Admin users can label and snip the audio clips and can perform DTW on the audio.
